Genius Academy For Genius People 2023

Students of Computer Science faculty from Kabul University has arranged a weekly panel under the name of Genius Academy For Genius People to assess the latest changes, developments and challenges on the field of Software Engineering.

2023 May 25th (Thu) - 8:00 AM ~ 1:00 pm (UTC+4:30).

@ Public Editorium of Kabul University

Panel Main Program

Opening

The panel starts with some opening talks Which include main topics and objectives of panel by moderator.

Lecture

Every panel member is appointed to talk and share their valueable thought for some 10 to 15 minutes.

Presentation

While a panelist is talking, the moderator presents his presentation to illustrate panelist's view with details.

Q & A

When panelists finish the lecture, participants can to their questions whether verbally or in a written manner.

Lunch

At the end of the program, organizers of panel arrange to deliver lunch for the participants of the panel.
